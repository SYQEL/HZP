# HZP — Hertz Player

**Retune any song to 432 Hz or the Solfeggio frequencies — in real time, on every device.**

HZP is an ad-free music player built around one idea: your music should play at *your* frequency. Shift any track from the 440 Hz concert‑pitch standard to 432 Hz or any Solfeggio frequency instantly — lossless, real‑time, no converting files by hand. Bring your own library, stream millions of tracks, and watch it all come alive with a built‑in visualizer.

## Features

- **🎚️ Real‑time retuning** — Play any song at 432 Hz or the full Solfeggio set (174–963 Hz), and return to the 440 Hz standard any time. The pitch shifts live as you listen — nothing to pre‑convert, nothing to wait for.
- **📁 Your local library** — Point HZP at your music folders and it builds a clean, VLC‑style catalog: browse by song, album, or artist, sort any column, and right‑click to queue, add to a playlist, or reveal in Finder. MP3, FLAC, M4A, WAV, and more — artwork and all.
- **🌐 Streaming built in** — Connect **Apple Music** to play your subscription, or stream **1,000,000+ tracks from Audius**. Retuning works across everything you play.
- **✨ SYQEL visualizer** — Real‑time, audio‑reactive visuals powered by SYQEL, right inside the player.
- **📚 Playlists & tools** — Playlists, favorites, a full listening history, and stats on what you play and the frequencies you reach for.
- **💻 Everywhere** — Native apps for **macOS** (Apple Silicon + Intel) and **Windows**, plus **web**, **iOS**, and **Android** — your library and playlists follow your account.
- **🚫 Ad‑free.**

## The frequencies

HZP plays your music at any of these tunings — pick the one you like and press play:

`174` · `285` · `396` · `417` · **`432`** · `528` · `639` · `741` · `852` · `963` Hz — plus **`440` Hz** (concert standard).

432 Hz and the Solfeggio frequencies are alternative tunings with a long musical and cultural history; HZP simply makes it effortless to hear any song in them.
